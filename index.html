[05] FAQ

Questions before starting a project

Short answers about scope, pricing, communication and what happens after you submit the enquiry form.

How do we start working with System Capital?

Start by submitting the project enquiry form. We review the business context, goals, service fit, budget range and timeline before recommending the next step.

Are the prices on the website final?

No. The packages are approximate starting points. Exact pricing depends on page count, design depth, integrations, content needs, support scope and launch requirements.

Can you handle both design and development?

Yes. The service model covers strategy, web design, frontend development, PHP form processing, launch checks and post-launch support.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes. Support can include content updates, technical checks, form monitoring, conversion improvements, analytics review and campaign-related page changes.

How is communication handled?

Initial communication starts through the structured form, then continues by email so project details, scope decisions and next steps stay clear.
